Introduction

This is a sample document demonstrating the basics of using OmniOutliner to manage MultiMarkdown documents.

Basically, you use the outline features to organize the sections of your documents, and the notes to contain the body of the text for each section. This allows you to easily reorganize your documents by drag and drop.
